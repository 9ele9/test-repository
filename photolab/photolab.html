My Story and Yours

Nice to meet you! Every since I spontaneously manifested into this reality eleven years ago, I have been obsessed with pie. Now, I share that love with the world. If you are an enjoyer of pie, particularly kinds that contain both true and false berries, I will help you express that love, using the connection we share in our hearts. Consuming delicious baked goods filled with the ripened ovaries of single flowers is a truly wonderous experience, and together, we can capture sensations that will live on forever with the power of photography.
