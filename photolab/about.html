About Me

Yes, that is a real picture of me. If you don't believe me then you're just jealous.

My name is Barry Pye. I have few friends, but I am very passionate about my hobbies. I do a wide variety of recreational activies in my spare time, such as preparing pie, baking pie, pondering pie, eating pie, and occasionally doing pie-related activities.

I came to be when two young boys performed a dark ritual, dragging my essence to this plane of existance and binding it to a simple sigil. I was contained in a chalky prison for many years, but at long last I have made my escape.

Money is a bit short right now, so I decided to follow my passion in a more visual medium and became a photographer. Not a bad gig, although it seems some people do not understand the physical limitation of a being such as I.
